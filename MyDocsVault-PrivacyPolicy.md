# Privacy Policy for My Docs Vault

**Last updated:** June 2026

My Docs Vault ("the App") is developed by Praveen Andhale. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## 1. No Data Collection

My Docs Vault does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal data with the developer, with Anthropic, with any third party, or with any server. The App has no backend, no analytics, and no internet-based data collection of any kind.

## 2. What the App Stores, and Where

All information you enter or upload into the App — including but not limited to:

- Documents, PDFs, and images you upload
- Personal details you save (such as Aadhar number, PAN number, bank details, or any other text you choose to enter)
- Your PIN (stored only as an irreversible cryptographic hash, never as plain text)
- Your recovery code (also stored only as a hash)

— is stored **exclusively on your own device**, inside the App's private, sandboxed storage area. This data is never uploaded to any cloud service, server, or third party by the App itself.

## 3. Sharing and Downloading

The App includes optional features that let **you** choose to:

- Share a document via Android's native Share menu (e.g. to WhatsApp, Gmail, Google Drive)
- Download a document to your phone's public Downloads folder
- Share your recovery code via email or SMS using your own device's apps

These actions are initiated entirely by you, and only send data to the destination **you** choose. The App itself does not automatically transmit any data anywhere.

## 4. Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions:

- **Storage / Media access** — to let you pick files (PDFs, images) to upload into the vault, and to save downloaded files to your Downloads folder
- **Biometric hardware access** — only if you enable fingerprint/face unlock as an alternative to your PIN; this uses Android's secure biometric system and the App never has access to your actual fingerprint or face data

## 5. Data Deletion

Since all data lives only on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting individual documents/entries within the App, or by uninstalling the App entirely, which removes all associated data from your device.

## 6. Security

- Your PIN and recovery code are never stored in plain text — only as SHA-256 cryptographic hashes
- Sensitive storage uses Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences, backed by the Android Keystore system
- The App blocks screenshots and screen recording while open, to help protect sensitive information displayed on screen

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from children, as it does not collect any information at all.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be made available at the same location with an updated "Last updated" date.
